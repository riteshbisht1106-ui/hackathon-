Unveiling the scale and magnitude of our cultural legacy.

India is home to 44 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, reflecting millennia of history.

⏳

Some heritage sites in India are over 2,000 years old, standing as timeless witnesses.

🌍

Heritage tourism attracts millions of visitors every year from across the globe.

💰

More Hidden Gems & Facts

Beyond the famous monuments lies a world of engineering marvels.

🧗

Kumbhalgarh Fort has the second-longest wall in the world after the Great Wall of China, extending over 36 km.

🛕

The Kailasa Temple at Ellora is the largest monolithic structure in the world, carved from a single rock top-down.

🌳

Meghalaya's Living Root Bridges are not built but grown by training rubber tree roots over centuries.

💧

Rani ki Vav is an inverted temple-style stepwell designed to emphasize the sanctity of water.
